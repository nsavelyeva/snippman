SnippMan is ...
A simple Snippets Manager with Web-interface for CRUD-operations, highlight.js as code highlighter, and SQL as a search engine. Read more.
Usage
  • Just launch run.py and open http://127.0.0.1 in your browser. Try "run.py -h" to see options.
  • Note: settings are loaded from config.py - feel free to customize them here.
  • Start your work - add language first and then you can add snippets.
